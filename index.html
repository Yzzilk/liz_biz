About

Our journey as accounting service matchmakers.

  • March 2016

    Idea

    Accountants are in high demand, yet we see a trend in people leaving the profession. Oftentimes, this trend affects women who are seeking more location and schedule flexibility. We set out on a mission to connect people in need of accounting services with freelance accountants helping fill a gap on both sides of the marketplace equation.

  • April 2016

    Formation

    Revwoo Inc. is formed with the goal of matching entrepreneurs with quality-vetted freelance accountants serving the Seattle area.

  • July 2016

    Customer Base Focus

    In order to better serve our customers, we decided to focus our marketing efforts on a more specific customer base.

  • September 2016

    Online Courses

    After six months of seeing the challenges and questions entrepreneurs had, we decided to address the most common questions for specific industries through online courses. This enables us to help more entrepreneurs and more efficiently meet the needs of our customers. We started out with content specific to LuLaRoe Consultants since we saw strong demand from this growing business.
